مرکزِ علم و حکمت

Jamia-Tun-Noor was established with a singular vision — to cultivate scholars who carry the light of Islamic knowledge into every corner of society. Grounded in the classical tradition of Dars-e-Nizami, our curriculum weaves together the wisdom of the ancients with the intellectual demands of the contemporary world.

Our institution has been a trusted sanctuary of learning for over two decades, producing Ulama, Huffaz, and community leaders who serve with integrity, compassion, and depth of understanding across Pakistan and beyond.

  • 📖
    Classical Curriculum Authentic Dars-e-Nizami methodology preserved and transmitted with scholarly rigour.
  • 🌿
    Holistic Development Character, spirituality, and intellectual excellence cultivated in equal measure.
  • 🤝
    Community Service Graduates serving masajid, madaris, and communities across the nation and globally.

Dars-e-Nizami

Our Core Sciences

Three pillars of Islamic scholarship, taught by senior Ulama in an environment dedicated to excellence, precision, and spiritual depth.

Jurisprudence
⚖️

Fiqh فقہ

A comprehensive study of Islamic jurisprudence — from foundational principles of Usul al-Fiqh to applied rulings across the Hanafi school. Students engage classical texts including Hidayah, Quduri, and Fatawa Alamgiri under senior Fuqaha.

  • 4 Years
  • Hanafi School
Learn More
Prophetic Tradition
📜

Hadith حدیث

An in-depth exploration of the Prophetic traditions — covering the sciences of Mustalah al-Hadith, chain criticism (Rijal), and the study of the six canonical collections (Kutub al-Sittah). The Daura-e-Hadith is the crown of the Nizami curriculum.

  • 3 Years
  • Kutub al-Sittah
Learn More
Quranic Exegesis
🌙

Tafseer تفسیر

A rigorous study of Quranic exegesis — encompassing linguistic analysis (I'rab), sciences of revelation (Ulum al-Quran), and classical commentaries including Tafsir Ibn Kathir, Jalalayn, and Bayan al-Quran.

  • 5 Years
  • Classical Texts
